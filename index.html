Typical Project Timelines
Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Smaller projects like landing pages take 2–4 weeks, while larger custom systems require 2–6 months. We use agile methodology to deliver functional MVP versions within the first 6–8 weeks so you can begin testing and providing feedback early.
Post-Launch Support & Maintenance
We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including 24/7 server monitoring, security patches, routine updates, and monthly performance audits. Our team remains your technical partner long after deployment, providing bug fixes and feature enhancements as your business evolves.
SaaS Architecture & Scalability
Our SaaS solutions are built on cloud-native architectures designed for infinite scalability. We implement multi-tenant databases and containerized deployments (Docker/Kubernetes) to ensure your platform remains responsive as your user base grows from hundreds to millions.
Legacy System Improvements
We specialize in modernizing outdated systems through incremental refactoring or complete cloud migration. By integrating modern APIs and optimizing database queries, we breathe new life into your existing infrastructure while reducing operational costs.
Do you build SaaS products?
Yes. We design and develop scalable SaaS products, customer portals, and subscription-based systems from the ground up — including billing integration, user authentication, multi-tenancy, and everything needed to launch a professional software product.
Where is Voltbit based?
Voltbit is based in Lusaka, Zambia. We serve clients locally across Zambia as well as businesses internationally, delivering high-performance digital solutions with the professional reliability your business deserves.
